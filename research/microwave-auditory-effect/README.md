# Microwave Auditory Effect: Sharp & Grove Research Trail

Research compiled May 2, 2026, tracing the citation chain from the 1973 Sharp & Grove demonstration at the Walter Reed Army Institute of Research through to modern Havana Syndrome discourse.

## Core finding

The famous "voice transmission via pulsed microwaves" demonstration is held up by a single citation chain: one personal communication (Sharp & Grove to Justesen, September 28, 1973), one synthesis paper (Justesen 1975 in *American Psychologist*), and one peer-reviewed acoustic-physics paper (Sharp/Grove/Gandhi 1974 in IEEE Trans. MTT) — **which does not discuss speech transmission at all**. Every modern account, including the 2026 Broughton synthesis, traces back to this same chain.

## Key facts established

1. **The 1974 IEEE paper used inanimate absorbers only.** Eccosorb WG4 polyurethane foam and crumpled aluminum foil. No tissue, no phantoms, no in vivo measurement. 1500 MHz carrier, 14 µs pulses, ~75 kW/m² peak — about 750× the contemporary 10 mW/cm² average safety limit.
2. **The speech demonstration was never published.** It exists only as Note 2 ("Personal communication, September 28, 1973") in Justesen 1975.
3. **Joseph C. Sharp (1934–2021) left the field in 1974.** Twenty years at NASA Ames, finishing as Director of the Office of Space Research. No post-1974 microwave publications. No public commentary on the demo or Havana Syndrome despite being alive until 2021.
4. **"Mark Grove" is unverified.** Primary sources call him H.M. Grove. He has no archival footprint after 1975.
5. **The Moscow Signal was continuous-wave.** This disqualifies it as a Frey-effect / MAE source — pulsed modulation is the entire mechanism. Citing Moscow as physical precedent for Havana misstates the underlying physics.
6. **Project Pandora was ended in 1969 after a RAND review demolished the data.** CIA's initial power-density measurements were 10–20× too high, which produced the early false positives.
7. **Lin vs. Foster, honestly assessed:** Lin has the better case for the *acute auditory phase* of Havana incidents matching MAE phenomenology. Foster has the better case that a covert injury-causing weapon at the reported geometries is implausible with publicly known technology.

## What this means for the Akwei investigation

The Sharp/Grove demonstration is one of the most-cited primary sources in synthetic-telepathy / V2K discourse. This research establishes:

- The physical phenomenon (microwave auditory effect via thermoelastic expansion) is real and replicated
- The specific speech-transmission claim has a single, thin source chain
- The lead investigator went silent for 47 years
- The peer-reviewed paper does not support what it is widely cited as supporting
- Modern weaponization claims at injury thresholds remain physically contested
