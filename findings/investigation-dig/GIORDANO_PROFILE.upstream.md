## Key Statements on Neuroweapons Technology

### On the Brain as a Target
> *"The brain is the new battlescape of the twenty-first century."*
> — Modern War Institute, West Point, 2017

> *"Neuroweapons are those things that can be used to work upon the peripheral and central nervous system in those ways that are going to be able to affect the cognitions, emotions, and behaviors of others in those ways that we desire them to be affected."*
> — TechNative interview, October 2025

### On Directed Energy and Neural Targeting
> *"The brain as an electromagnetic organ is therefore sensitive to a variety of energies that may be directable... we're talking about directed energy weapons and/or directed energy devices that could be weaponized. We're looking at a broad spectrum — direct and rangeable acoustics and sonics, ultrasound and infrasound."*
> — TechNative, October 2025

### On AI Neural Signal Decoding
> *"We're able to reconstruct various visual stimuli, auditory stimuli, tactile stimuli that an individual is exposed to or is feeling based upon a pattern reconstruction of a neuroimage of their brain... we've been doing this work for about 20 years."*
> — TechNative, October 2025

### On DARPA N3 Nanotransducers
> *"The other approach showing considerable progress is the use of very very small-scale sensors and transmitters that can be input to the brain non-surgically... via inhalation, injection, ingestion, absorption through a mucous membrane, migrated into the brain space utilizing some form of magnetic current."*

> *"Could they in fact be inflicted upon an individual without this individual knowing? Well, yeah, actually they could."*
> — TechNative, October 2025

### On the Progressive Neurological Injury Cascade
> *"The initial insult at the injury itself is nothing more than the first snowball that then leads to the avalanche. The injury to the brain impairs the brain's ability to not only function at the time of the injury and thereafter, but to compensate effectively. So then you begin to see a cascade of effects."*
> — Catherine Herridge investigation, TreatNOW, April 2025

### On Nanomaterials as Covert Disruption Tools
> *"Nanomaterials for rather crude but certainly capable clandestine if not covert weapons that are able to disrupt various aspects and domains of brain function... instruments of very specific disruption... to disrupt particular types of function in a very selected key target, a high value target."*
> — TechNative, October 2025

### On Detection and Defense
> *"There are ways of detecting that these types of things are operational — environmental detection, architectural detection, personal detection. And then once these things are being detected, at that point we can then deter them."*
