# T2 / T4 / Case-Attribution Closure Audit

Date: April 2026

Objective: test whether current open records close these three bars:
1) T2 hard efficacy closure (operational semantic speech performance),
2) T4 deployment closure (fielded/used as alleged),
3) specific actor -> specific person -> specific case attribution.

## A) T2 hard efficacy closure

## Closure standard

Needs protocol-grade evidence with:
- intelligible speech endpoint (not just clicks/buzz),
- reproducible results across subjects/sites,
- blinded/sham-controlled design and pre-specified metrics,
- dose/exposure and safety margins sufficient for operational claims.

## Current evidence status

- Reviews and modeling continue to support MAE mechanism plausibility and threshold discussions, but emphasize sparse data and large uncertainty for weaponized/intelligible outcomes.
- Patent-language claims remain engineering-claim space, not efficacy closure.

## Judgment

`T2 NOT CLOSED` in current open corpus.

## B) T4 deployment closure

## Closure standard

Needs primary records showing:
- identified system of record or validated field prototype,
- operational test outcomes against mission requirements,
- documented deployment/use (unit, timeframe, mission context),
- chain from test article to real-world use claim.

## Current evidence status

- Public DOT&E/DoD materials confirm broad directed-energy testing ecosystems and prototyping activity.
- GAO reporting on AHI still states no definitive who/what cause closure.
- No recovered primary record in this pass establishes a publicly documented MAE semantic-speech system fielded and used as alleged.

## Judgment

`T4 NOT CLOSED` in current open corpus.

## C) Specific actor -> specific person -> specific case attribution

## Closure standard

For U.S. litigation-grade posture, this generally requires:
- admissible expert methodology under Rule 702,
- authenticated technical evidence under Rule 901,
- both general causation and specific causation support,
- exclusion of plausible alternatives (or rigorous differential etiology),
- actor-link evidence (custody/control, capability, temporal/spatial nexus).

## Current evidence status

- Legal standards and evidentiary rules are clear on reliability/authentication thresholds.
- No open evidentiary package in this pass closes the actor-person-case chain.

## Judgment

`CASE-SPECIFIC ATTRIBUTION NOT CLOSED` in current open corpus.

## Net result

Across all three requested closure bars:
- T2 hard efficacy: open
- T4 deployment: open
- specific actor/person/case attribution: open

Best current use of available evidence remains:
- mechanism and intent/provenance support (T1/T3),
- with explicit non-closure language for T2/T4 and case-specific attribution.
