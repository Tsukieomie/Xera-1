## 2) Three parallel “speech-adjacent” tracks (do not merge)

### Track A — **Tier A** microwave hearing / thermoacoustic (“hearing effect”)

**FY74 WU 057** (`ADA009338_…`, “Modulated microwave signals” subsection, printed pp. 985–986): completed studies on the **so-called hearing effect** at very low average power; work paused for transducer modeling + BBB concern; ties to publication **Sharp, Grove, Gandhi 1974 IEEE T-MTT** (acoustic signals from pulsed microwaves) — **mechanism**, not word intelligibility.

### Track B — **Tier B** alleged nine-word / voice-modulated demonstration (Sept 1973)

**Justesen 1975** *American Psychologist* — published secondary report citing **`Sharp, J. C., & Grove, M. Personal communication, September 28, 1973`**. No matching methods paragraph in FY74 APR text; no Sharp/Grove speech protocol in open DTIC under joint authorship (`SHARP_GROVE_PRIMARY_REPORT_HUNT.md`).

### Track C — **Same project number, explicit speech-induction failure (1972)**

**Frey / Messenger / Eichert**, June 1972, NTIS **AD-747684** (`DTIC_AD0747684_djvu.txt`), **project `1J662712AJ21`** (same as Hawkins/Grove March 1973 report). Phase three abstract: attempted **perception of speech** via RF; **vocoder approach did not generate speech**; **periodicity-pitch rules did not yield perceived RF speech**. This is a **documented negative** on one speech-encoding strategy *before* the Sept 1973 anecdote — it does **not** refute Justesen’s separate anecdote, but it **tightens** the institutional story: the project line was already experimenting with “speech” framing and reporting **failure** in the open NTIS record.

## 4) FY74 WU 057 vs Tier B (one-paragraph discipline)

FY74 narrative describes **modulated microwaves**, **“hearing effect,”** **“auditory study,”** cessation reasons, and links to the **1974 IEEE T-MTT** mechanism paper. It does **not** describe digit words, voiceprints, intelligibility scoring, or Justesen’s visit.

**Interpretation already in `FY74_SPEECH_DEMO_HUNT.md`:** FY74 **corroborates auditory / MAE-context work** in the window that contains 28 Sept 1973; it does **not** corroborate the **escalated** Justesen narrative (recorded voice → microwave → nine intelligible words) as an **Army-reported** program output.
