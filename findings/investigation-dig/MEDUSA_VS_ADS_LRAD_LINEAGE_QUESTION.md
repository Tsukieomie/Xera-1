## MEDUSA vs ADS vs LRAD — lineage question, direct answer

Date: April 2026

Scope: this note exists to answer one specific public-record question that
keeps coming up around WaveBand MEDUSA: **are ADS and LRAD (or the broader
acoustic-hailing device family) evolutions or variations of MEDUSA?** The
answer is supplied here in one place so that readers do not have to re-derive
it from `MEDUSA_DEEP_AUDIT.md`, `ADS_JNLWP_DEEP_DIVE_CONTRAST_NOTE.md`, and
`ADS_JNLWP_DEEP_DIVE_CONTRAST_NOTE_SUPPLEMENT.md` individually.

This note is interpretive and source-disciplined. It does not introduce new
primary evidence; it consolidates what is already established in the
neighbouring notes and the cited public sources, and frames the answer in the
form most likely to be misread otherwise.

### 1) Short answer

**No.** ADS and LRAD / acoustic hailing are **not** evolutions or variants of
WaveBand MEDUSA.

- **ADS** (Active Denial System) is a JNLWP / JNLWD 95 GHz millimeter-wave
  skin-heating / repel system.
- **LRAD / AHD** (Long Range Acoustic Device / Acoustic Hailing Device) is a
  fielded audible acoustic-hailing / voice-and-tone projection family — it
  produces directional sound through air, not RF in the head.
- **WaveBand MEDUSA** (Mob Excess Deterrent Using Silent Audio) was a Navy
  SBIR Phase I breadboard prototype based on the microwave auditory effect
  (MAE) — i.e., specifically pulsed microwaves creating a sound sensation
  inside the head.

The three paths share the broad non-lethal / counter-personnel problem
space. The public evidence trail does **not** support a lineage claim from
MEDUSA to ADS or to LRAD / acoustic hailing. They are best described as
*adjacent capability families inside the same broader NLW ecosystem*, not as
parent and child programs.

### 2) Why the lineage claim does not hold — three independent dimensions

| Dimension | WaveBand MEDUSA | ADS | LRAD / Acoustic Hailing (AHD family) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Funding / program path | Navy SBIR Phase I, contract `M67854-04-C-1012`, topic `N03-163`, solicitation `03.2`, firm WaveBand Corp, Dr. Lev Sadovnik. No WaveBand Phase II transition surfaced. | JNLWP / JNLWD ACTD 2002-2007, post-ACTD bridge program under the Air Armament Center, multi-year DoD NLWP capability line; HEAP human-effects review cycle. | Fielded COTS-derived force-protection acquisition; AHDs cited in DoD NLWP Annual Reviews from 2010-2015 and in JNLWD reference materials; named devices include LRAD, MRAD, Hyperspike, MAD, Mini-DSLA, HALLTS, Magnetic Acoustic / Audio Device. |
| Mechanism | Microwave auditory effect / "silent audio" — pulsed microwaves create a sound sensation in the human head. | 95 GHz millimeter-wave energy; penetrates ~1/64 inch into skin; intense heating → repel response. (Some materials cite 94 GHz; recorded as-stated.) | Audible / mechanical air-pressure sound; scalable directional warning tones or intelligible voice commands at >500 m; not RF, not MAE. |
| Status / public evidence trail | Phase I breadboard prototype, contractor self-report only. No deployment, no JNLWP transition record, no HEAP-class human-effects review, no annual-report citation found through 2015. | Documented multi-year transition / safety / legal-review trail; ACTD, JMUAs, extended user evaluations, ADS1 / ADS2 platforms, Quantico 2012 / Twentynine Palms / Joint Base Langley-Fort Eustis demonstrations. | Documented fielded use; Navy use of AHDs cited from 2006 onward; appears across multiple JNLWD / DoD NLWP public documents. |

Each of those three dimensions — funding path, mechanism, and evidence trail
— is independently sufficient to refuse a "MEDUSA → ADS" or
"MEDUSA → LRAD" lineage claim. ADS does not inherit MEDUSA's MAE mechanism
or its SBIR funding line. LRAD / AHD does not even operate in the
electromagnetic spectrum.

### 4) Common confusion points to keep separate

These conflations show up in secondary commentary about MEDUSA. None of them
are supported by the public record reviewed in this corpus.

- **"GD-OTS Medusa" → WaveBand MEDUSA.** General Dynamics — Ordnance and
  Tactical Systems was awarded a 2010 Mission Payload Module Non-Lethal
  Weapon System (MPM-NLWS) "Medusa Weapon System" contract using 66 mm
  grenade / launcher technology and light / sound / pressure effects. That
  is a different program, with different sponsor, different mechanism, and
  different vendor. It is **not** the WaveBand MAE concept.
  Source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/general-dynamics-awarded-mission-payload-module-technology-demonstration-contract-by-us-marine-corps-92505614.html
- **"LRAD = MEDUSA in the head."** LRAD and the broader AHD family produce
  air-pressure acoustic energy; perception is via normal hearing through
  the ear. MEDUSA's claimed mechanism is RF-induced thermoelastic expansion
  in head tissues (microwave auditory effect). Treating LRAD as "MEDUSA
  evolved" collapses two different physical mechanisms.
- **"ADS heats the head, MEDUSA puts sound in the head, therefore one
  evolved from the other."** ADS is explicitly characterized in JNLWP
  materials as 95 GHz with ~1/64 inch coupling depth and a heat / repel
  effect — and the JNLWP materials explicitly distinguish 95 GHz mm-wave
  from microwave. The two mechanisms target different tissue depths,
  different sensations, and different operational outcomes; co-membership
  in "directed-energy NLW" is not lineage.
- **"Common JNLWP umbrella implies common ancestry."** ADS and AHDs both
  appear under JNLWP / DoD NLWP. WaveBand MEDUSA does not. Even if it had,
  shared sponsor would not establish technical lineage.

### 5) What the public record does support

- ADS, AHD, and (separately) the WaveBand MEDUSA SBIR Phase I concept all
  sit inside the broader **non-lethal / counter-personnel ecosystem** that
  JNLWP / DoD NLWP coordinates and reports on.
- It is fair to describe them as *adjacent* — same problem space, same
  general decade, similar high-level policy framing ("between shouting and
  shooting").
- It is **not** fair, on the public record reviewed here, to call any of
  them an evolution, variant, successor, or transition path of any of the
  others.

### 6) What would change this conclusion

This note phrases its conclusion as a public-evidence statement, not as an
absolute impossibility claim. The conclusion would update if any of the
following surfaced:

- A primary record placing WaveBand MEDUSA technology, IP, personnel, or
  funding inside the ADS or LRAD / AHD program lines.
- A JNLWP / JNLWD / DoD NLWP document naming WaveBand MEDUSA as an input,
  predecessor, or transition to ADS or AHD.
- A HEAP or HEAP-equivalent independent human-effects review of the
  WaveBand MEDUSA concept tied to the ADS or LRAD line.
- A Phase II / Phase III SBIR record or other DoD funding line carrying
  WaveBand MEDUSA forward and intersecting an ADS or LRAD program element.

Until then, the public-evidence answer to "is ADS / LRAD an evolution or
variation of MEDUSA?" is **no**.
