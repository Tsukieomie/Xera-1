# Deep-Dive ADS / JNLWP Contrast Note

Date: April 2026

Scope: extract the major substantive points from the accessible 2006-2008 JNLWP/ADS public documents (see `JNLWP_2006_2008_NEGATIVE_CONTRAST_NOTE.md` for the document list and target-term scan), and use them to draw a disciplined contrast between the **Active Denial System (ADS)** public trail and the **WaveBand MEDUSA** public trail.

This note is interpretive. It does not change conclusions about MEDUSA beyond what the public record supports.

## 1) Major points extracted from the ADS / JNLWP documents

These are the recurring substantive points across the 2006 brief, 2007 brief, the human-effects tri-fold, and the two ADS FAQs:

- **Doctrinal framing**: ADS is positioned to fill the non-lethal weapons (NLW) gap "between shouting and shooting" — i.e., a counter-personnel capability between verbal commands and lethal force.
- **System type**: ADS is a mobile directed-energy counter-personnel system.
- **Frequency**: 95 GHz millimeter-wave. The materials explicitly characterize this as **not microwave**.
- **Coupling depth**: Energy is absorbed in approximately the top **1/64 inch (~0.4 mm)** of skin.
- **Effect**: An instantaneous repelling **heat / pain sensation**, with no claim of weaponized cognitive or auditory effect.
- **Bioeffects research lineage**: 12+ years of bioeffects research is cited as the safety basis.
- **Exposure counts (cumulative narrative across documents)**:
  - 2006 brief: 9,300+ exposures
  - 2007 brief: 10,000+ exposures
  - 2008 FAQ: 11,000+ exposures
- **Independent oversight**: independent review boards are cited as part of the safety / human-effects governance posture.
- **Acknowledged incidents**:
  - January 1999 lab mishap producing a reported second-degree burn during testing.
  - April 2007 Moody AFB training overexposure.
- **Mitigations**: subsequent control / software / procedural changes are cited as response to the acknowledged incidents.
- **Acquisition phase**:
  - Advanced Concept Technology Demonstration (ACTD) phase **2002-2007**.
  - Post-ACTD **bridge program** to sustain the capability between formal program phases.

## 2) What the ADS public trail demonstrates as a baseline

The ADS materials show what a *publicly visible non-lethal directed-energy program with a real transition arc* looks like in JNLWP communications:

- A multi-year ACTD with named start/end dates.
- A bridge program after ACTD.
- A cumulative human-exposure record openly reported and updated across years.
- Named incidents and named mitigations.
- A specific, narrowly framed bio-effect (skin heating) with a specific frequency band.
- Reference to independent review boards and a legal/treaty review process.

This is a **complete public transition / safety / legal review narrative**.

## 3) The interpretive contrast with WaveBand MEDUSA

In the same JNLWP corpus, **none of the MEDUSA target terms appear** (per `JNLWP_2006_2008_NEGATIVE_CONTRAST_NOTE.md`).

In the broader open MEDUSA corpus already in this repo:

- The strongest primary anchor remains the archived Navy SBIR Phase I summary record (`medusa-navysbir-wayback.html`) for `M67854-04-C-1012`, topic `N03-163`, firm WaveBand Corporation.
- The publicly indexed Phase II continuation under topic `N03-163` belongs to the **EPIC / Invocon** line, **not** WaveBand MEDUSA (per `MEDUSA_TRANSITION_GAP_ANALYSIS.md`).
- No JNLWP transition/safety/legal-review narrative for MEDUSA appears in the accessible 2006-2008 JNLWP materials.

Stated symmetrically:

## 4) Interpretive point

The JNLWP public trail in 2006-2008 contains a full transition / safety / legal-review narrative for ADS, but no analogous public JNLWP trace for WaveBand MEDUSA.

This pattern is consistent with classifying MEDUSA as **SBIR Phase I / contractor self-report only** — not a JNLWP-transitioned, operationalized, or fielded capability.

This is not a claim that MEDUSA was "debunked" or that it never produced any technical output. It is a claim about the **shape of the public record**: ADS produced a recognizable program-of-record signature in JNLWP's public materials; MEDUSA did not.

## 6.1) Lineage-question recap

A direct answer to the recurring question "are ADS and LRAD evolutions or variations of WaveBand MEDUSA?" is: **no.** ADS is JNLWP / JNLWD 95 GHz millimeter-wave skin-heating; LRAD / AHD is fielded audible acoustic-hailing / voice-and-tone projection; WaveBand MEDUSA was a Navy SBIR Phase I microwave-auditory-effect breadboard prototype. Same broad non-lethal / counter-personnel ecosystem; different funding paths, different mechanisms, disjoint public evidence trails. "Adjacent" is fair; "evolution / variant / successor / transition path" is not supported by the public record. Consolidated write-up: `MEDUSA_VS_ADS_LRAD_LINEAGE_QUESTION.md`.
