## C) Overall conclusion - replace entire paragraph

Replace any version of:
"The complete record ... establishes that Voice-to-Skull technology is real ... portable operational device ... confirmed by Pentagon laboratory testing."

With:
"The open record strongly supports microwave auditory-effect mechanism plausibility (`T1`) and documents sustained patent/program interest (`T3`). Public evidence remains insufficient to establish protocol-grade covert semantic speech delivery (`T2`) or case-specific operational attribution (`T4`). Legal and policy developments are materially relevant context (`T5`) but do not, by themselves, close the technical proof gap."
