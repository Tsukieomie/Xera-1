## Status by sub-task

### 1. WRAIR Work Unit 057 (FY73 / FY74) — partially confirmed

- WU 057 **was found** inside DTIC accession **ADA009338**, the WRAIR annual progress report covering **1 July 1973 – 30 June 1974**.
- The official work-unit title is **"Biological effects and hazards of microwave radiation"**, *not* literally "Behavioral Biology." It sits under Project 3A762758A824, Task 01, "Radiation Injury and Protection."
- The section nevertheless describes **behavioral and biological microwave effects** (mouse and rat lethality at 2.45 GHz and 1.7 GHz, E/H vector orientation effects, pulsed-microwave hearing effect, blood-brain barrier observations, ocular studies) and explicitly references FY73 rat work, so it plausibly overlaps the requester's "behavioral biology" framing.
- PI: **Joseph C. Sharp, Ph.D.** Associates listed: H. Mark Grove, Sandra H. Githens, T. Daryl Hawkins, MAJ Stuart E. Hirsch, John H. Jacobi, John F. Schrot, Peter Brown.
- The raw extract is saved at `docs/research/wrair-nsa-yearbooks/wrair-wu057-extract.txt`.
- **Resume from here:** treat the extract as the confirmed WU 057 section. To pull the FY73 *predecessor* report (the one actually titled around behavioral biology, if it exists), request box / folder holdings from the **WRAIR Gorgas Memorial Library** archives, Series III "Publications and Reports" (the public finding aid lists 1978 Work Unit Summaries but not FY73 explicitly).

### 3. "Northwand High School" / NorthWood High School yearbooks — partially checked

- **"Northwand High School" was not confirmed** as a real school name.
- The most likely intended school, **NorthWood High School in Nappanee, Indiana**, has yearbooks on Internet Archive for **1978, 1979, 1980, and 1981**.
- **1980 and 1981** volumes were OCR-searched for: Engel, Engels, NSA, WRAIR, Oscar, and related terms — **all negative**.
- **1978 and 1979** volumes are present as image scans and **still need OCR** before they can be searched the same way.
- **Resume from here:** (a) run OCR on the 1978 and 1979 NorthWood volumes and repeat the term list; (b) independently confirm whether the requester actually meant "NorthWood" or a different school whose name only resembles "Northwand."

### 4. "Oscar 1980" Brain Research follow-up — not verified

- **No confirmed "Oscar 1980" follow-up paper in the journal *Brain Research* was located.**
- Adjacent 1980-era microwave bioeffects leads exist but are in **other journals**: James C. Lin on microwave / blood-brain barrier in *Bioelectromagnetics*, and a 1980 microwave auditory phenomenon paper in *Proceedings of the IEEE*. These are not in *Brain Research*.
- **Resume from here:** clarify what "Oscar" denotes — person, lab nickname, acronym, paper title, or codeword — then search by author / title / DOI. Without disambiguation, this lead cannot be advanced further inside *Brain Research*.
